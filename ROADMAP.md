# Saunterby — Roadmap

Where we are, and where we're headed. Plans evolve as we learn from team and members.

---

## Right now

Early days, road testing and having conversations. :)

## Coming up

As we grow, our focus is on:

- **Deepening what's already working** — more activities, more thoughtful accessibility options, more ways to use benefits you already have
- **Making booking easier**, without losing the personal touch that makes Saunterby feel different from an app
- **Growing thoughtfully** — applying community and stakeholder feedback.

## Down the road

Longer-term, we're interested in:

- Formal partnerships with activity providers aligned with our mission
- Deeper integration with benefits programs, so using what you already have becomes more and more convenient
- Deepening the Saunterby model
